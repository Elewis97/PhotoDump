About Photo Dump

You get invited to a major event. It was awesome! There were so many people and the entertainment was extraordinary. Everyone was trying to capture the moment. iPhones were out, professional photographers were capturing the scene, and you had caught many beautiful moments yourself… But you didn’t catch all of it. Unfortunately for you, in order to get the photos everyone else took, you have to remember who went, who took pictures, and find each of their individual profile pages to find those noteworthy photos.

But not any more!

With PhotoDump, everyone shares all of their photos onto a group page. One location, one place for all of the captured memories from that awesome day.
